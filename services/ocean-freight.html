Our Ocean Freight Services

Comprehensive solutions tailored to your shipping needs

Full Container Load (FCL)

Dedicated container shipping for large volume cargo, offering cost-effective and secure transportation for your exclusive use.

  • 20ft, 40ft, and 40ft High Cube containers
  • Direct routing options
  • Sealed container security
  • Door-to-door service available

Less than Container Load (LCL)

Flexible shipping solutions for smaller cargo volumes, allowing you to pay only for the space you need.

  • Cost-effective for smaller shipments
  • Regular consolidation services
  • Flexible volume options
  • Worldwide destinations
